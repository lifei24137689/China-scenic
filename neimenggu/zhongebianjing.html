The Majestic Gateway

The Manzhouli National Gate is China's largest land port gateway, standing 30 meters tall and 40 meters wide, a sight of grand majesty. This imposing gate rises on the China-Russia border, serving as an important symbol of the nation's northern portal. Ascending the gate's observation deck offers a panoramic view of Zabaikalsk, Russia, letting visitors experience the unique atmosphere where two countries meet.

The gate area also preserves Boundary Marker No. 41, a key China-Russia border monument established in 1993. On both sides of the marker, the railways of China and Russia converge, with international trains departing from here toward Moscow. This site is recognized as a vital node on the "Eurasian Continental Bridge," drawing millions of visitors each year to witness the grandeur of the national gate.

Matryoshka Square

Matryoshka Square is Manzhouli's iconic cultural landmark, themed around the Russian nesting doll and blending cultural elements from China, Russia, and Mongolia. At the center of the square stands a giant matryoshka building, approximately 30 meters tall — the largest matryoshka-shaped structure in the world. Its exterior features exquisite painted artwork, making it a must-visit photo destination for travelers.

Surrounding the square are various matryoshka sculptures and painted art installations, each telling a different cultural story. After nightfall, a light show illuminates the square in a riot of colors, while Russian cultural performances and Mongolian song and dance complement each other, showcasing the unique charm of three cultures in harmony.

Border Trade

The China-Russia Border Trade Zone is the core functional area of Manzhouli as the "Window of East Asia" and one of China's largest border trade zones. It brings together a wide variety of goods from Russia, Mongolia, and China — from Russian matryoshka dolls, chocolate, and amethyst jewelry to Mongolian furs and cashmere products, creating a dazzling shopping experience.

The trade zone also features a duty-free shopping center and cross-border e-commerce experience stores, where visitors can purchase authentic Russian products. The annual China-Russia-Mongolia International Ice & Snow Festival and beauty pageant further elevate the cultural exchange among the three nations, infusing this border city with vibrant energy. The Wedding Palace, as Manzhouli's romantic landmark, is also a popular venue for couples taking wedding photos.
