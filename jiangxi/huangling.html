◈ Sun-Drying Harvest Brief

Huangling, perched on the mountainside of Shier Mountain in Wuyuan County, Shangrao City, Jiangxi Province, is an "ancient Huizhou village suspended on a cliff." Founded in the mid-Ming dynasty, it has a history of nearly 600 years. It is world-famous for "sun-drying the harvest" — red peppers, golden chrysanthemums, and yellow corn spread across white walls and black tiles, hailed as "China's Most Beautiful Symbol." In 2024, it was elevated to a National 5A Scenic Area.

/// 01

China's Most Beautiful Symbol

The ancient village of Huangling sits on rugged terrain where "there is not three feet of flat land." The ancestors used bamboo baskets on rooftops and windowsills to dry crops across a hundred-meter vertical drop, gradually forming the unique "sun-drying harvest" folk custom. Today, this tradition has endured for centuries, becoming Wuyuan's most renowned cultural landscape.

In spring, mountain ferns and bamboo shoots are dried; in summer, vegetables and chili peppers; in autumn, fruits and imperial chrysanthemums; in winter, preserved meats and dried fruits — as the seasons turn, the colors on the bamboo baskets change as well. The annual Sun-Drying Harvest Cultural Festival held at the start of autumn features harvest parades and intangible cultural heritage showcases, drawing visitors from around the world.

Huizhou Aesthetics on the Cliff

The ancient village of Huangling is arranged in a fan-shaped, terraced layout around the water gap, with white walls, black tiles, and horse-head walls rising and falling — a quintessential Huizhou mountain village. The "Three Carvings of Huizhou" — wood carving, brick carving, and stone carving — are found throughout, with the Yixin Building featuring exquisite carved beams and the Zhuxu Hall showcasing delicate craftsmanship, each a masterful work of art.

The scenic area has acquired over 20 scattered Huizhou ancient buildings from various regions for off-site preservation and reconstruction, making Huangling a "museum of Huizhou architecture without walls." Heavenly Street is the village's main thoroughfare, lined with Huizhou-style shops, resembling a living scroll of "Along the River During the Qingming Festival."

Poetic Pastoral in Every Season

The beauty of Huangling has its own charm in every season. In spring, the vast terraced rapeseed flower sea blooms in brilliant gold, complementing the white walls and black tiles; in midsummer, lush green trees provide shade, and the Flower Stream Street offers cool refreshment; in golden autumn, the sun-drying harvest is in full swing, with red peppers and golden chrysanthemums covering roofs and tiles in a colorful palette; in winter, the aroma of preserved meats fills the air, and the ancient village radiates tranquility under the warm sun.

The Leixin Bridge spans across the valley, offering panoramic views of the terraced fields and the ancient village from above. The Flower Stream Street winds its way with babbling brooks and lush foliage, making it a perfect spot for photos. The Glowing Forest light show at night adds a dreamy touch to the ancient village.
