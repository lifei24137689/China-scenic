Mount Sanbai

Jiangxi Province · Ganzhou City

This is the source of the Dongjiang River, the origin of drinking water for our compatriots in Hong Kong. Chapter One

Source of the Dongjiang · Water of Life

Mount Sanbai is the birthplace of the Dongjiang River, one of the three major tributaries of the Pearl River. It flows through Heyuan, Huizhou, and Dongguan in Guangdong, eventually reaching the Pearl River Estuary. The Dongjiang water supply project delivers hundreds of millions of cubic meters of freshwater to Hong Kong each year, with the clear springs of Mount Sanbai nourishing tens of millions of people in the Greater Bay Area.

At Fu'ao Pond on Mount Sanbai, crystal-clear spring water gushes from the mountains, gathering into streams that rush southward. This is the starting point of the Dongjiang River and a spiritual homeland where Hong Kong compatriots remember the source of their water. Every year, many Hong Kong tourists make a special trip to Mount Sanbai to visit the source of the Dongjiang.

"How can a canal be so clear? Only because there is fresh water from the source — Mount Sanbai, the origin of the Dongjiang, the spring of life."
— Mount Sanbai · Source of the Dongjiang
Chapter Two

Primeval Forest · Ecological Sanctuary

Mount Sanbai boasts a forest coverage rate of 98%, one of the highest in Jiangxi Province. Vast expanses of ancient secondary forests are preserved here, with towering ancient trees, dense vines, and extremely high concentrations of negative oxygen ions — truly a "natural oxygen bar."

The scenic area is exceptionally rich in flora and fauna, featuring unique landscapes such as Volcanic Heavenly Pool and Zhiyin Spring. The volcanic landform remnants bear witness to geological changes hundreds of millions of years ago, while the primeval forest is home to numerous rare species, making it a treasure trove of biodiversity.

Chapter Three

Three Wonders · Headwater Waterfall

Mount Sanbai is famous for its "Three Wonders": Headwater Waterfall, Primeval Forest, and Volcanic Landforms. Among them, the Dongjiang First Waterfall drops nearly a hundred meters, cascading down with tremendous force — the most spectacular waterfall on Mount Sanbai. The waterfall's waters are the very headwaters of the Dongjiang River.

In addition, Dongfeng Lake shimmers with emerald waves, its mountain and lake views complementing each other; the waters of Zhiyin Spring patter like a lute or zither. Every landscape on Mount Sanbai tells the story of nature's wonder and magnificence, making it a rare ecological sanctuary for the Greater Bay Area.
