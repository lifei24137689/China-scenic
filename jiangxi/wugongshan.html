Wugong Mountain

Pingxiang · Jiangxi Province

100,000 mu of alpine meadows stretch along the ridges above 1,600 meters, acclaimed as the "Meadow in the Clouds." Golden Summit sunrise, sea of clouds wonders, and alpine meadows — the three marvels of Wugong Mountain, making it China's most beautiful hiking paradise.

Chapter One

Golden Summit Sunrise · Above the Sea of Clouds

Golden Summit, at 1,918.3 meters above sea level, is the main peak of Wugong Mountain and the highest peak in Jiangxi Province. Standing atop the summit, clouds roll beneath your feet while distant mountains loom in shades of indigo. As dawn breaks, golden sunlight pierces through the clouds and spills across the meadows, painting the entire world in gold.

The sunrise from Golden Summit is hailed as one of the most beautiful in East China. In the twilight hours, a faint orange glow gradually emerges on the horizon, then the entire eastern sky blazes into vibrant colors as the red sun leaps above the sea of clouds — a breathtaking spectacle.

"The sky is vast, the plains are wide — wind bends the grass, revealing cattle and sheep. You need not travel to the grasslands; here at Wugong Mountain, you'll find this boundless grandeur."
—— Wugong Mountain · Meadow in the Clouds
Chapter Two

Vast Meadows · Walking in the Clouds

Wugong Mountain boasts a rare expanse of alpine meadows in southern China, covering over 100,000 mu and stretching continuously along the ridgeline above 1,600 meters. In spring and summer, the meadows are a lush green carpet; in autumn and winter, they turn golden, offering a different kind of majestic beauty.

The Fayunjie to Golden Summit traverse is one of China's most classic hiking routes. Walking along the grassy ridgeline with deep abysses and rolling seas of clouds on both sides, you feel as though you are strolling across the horizon — a truly exhilarating experience.

Chapter Three

Outdoor Paradise · Tent Rendezvous

Wugong Mountain is a paradise for outdoor sports enthusiasts. Every year during summer and autumn, thousands of outdoor lovers flock here, pitching tents on Golden Summit meadows to await the sunrise and sea of clouds. The Wugong Mountain Tent Festival has become one of the largest outdoor camping events in China.

The infamous Despair Slope lives up to its name — but when you conquer it and reach Golden Summit, all exhaustion transforms into the joy of reaching the top. This is an essential trial for every Wugong Mountain hiker, and an unforgettable journey.
