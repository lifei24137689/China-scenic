/// 01

Green Lung of North China · Eco Pearl

Hengshui Lake Scenic Area covers 26.4 km², connected by the Hengshui Lake Marathon lakeside track through ecological experience zones and historical & cultural areas. It is one of the largest freshwater lakes on the North China Plain, renowned as the "Most Beautiful Wetland of Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei."

The scenic area features vast waters with rippling blue waves — islands dot the lake, birds inhabit the islands, and farmland borders the shores, forming a unique "lake-island-farmland-forest" four-in-one ecological landscape. It is a rare natural oxygen bar in North China and a newly designated national 5A scenic area in 2024.

/// 02

Bird Paradise · Wetland Wonderland

Hengshui Lake is a national nature reserve, home to the best-preserved freshwater wetland ecosystem on the North China Plain. Over 300 bird species inhabit this area, including nationally protected first-class birds such as red-crowned cranes, Siberian white cranes, Oriental storks, and great bustards. It serves as a vital stopover on the global migratory bird route.

Every spring and autumn, tens of thousands of migratory birds stop here to rest and feed. White egrets glide gracefully and swans swim across the lake, creating a magnificent scene reminiscent of the classic verse "sunset clouds fly with a lone duck, autumn waters share the same hue with the vast sky." The area features bird-watching platforms and a wetland science museum, making it an ideal destination for birding enthusiasts and nature learners.

/// 03

Lakeside Track · Waterfront Views

The Hengshui Lake Marathon track winds around the lake, stretching approximately 42 km — one of the few lakeside marathon tracks in China. The route meanders between lake vistas and shaded greenery, allowing runners to enjoy the refreshing lake breeze and the harmonious blend of water and sky.

Along the track, multiple ecological experience zones and rest stations are available. Visitors can cycle, stroll, or take sightseeing boats to explore the lake area. The lotus ponds, reed marshes, and aquatic forests along the shore create a series of stunning natural paintings, offering different scenic experiences across all four seasons — an ideal destination for leisure vacations and water sports.
