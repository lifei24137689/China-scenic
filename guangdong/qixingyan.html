Lingnan's Top Marvel

Qixingyan — the Top Marvel of Lingnan. Seven limestone peaks are arranged like the Big Dipper upon the emerald waters of Star Lake, creating a unique karst landscape where "mountains rise from waters, waters flow among mountains," acclaimed as the "Top Marvel of Lingnan."

Marshal Ye Jianying composed a poem after visiting Qixingyan: "Borrowing a circle of West Lake's water, and moving seven hills from Yangshuo. Adding willows along the banks, leaving a timeless painting between heaven and earth." He likened Qixingyan to a perfect fusion of West Lake's waters and Yangshuo's peaks. Together with Dinghu Mountain, Qixingyan forms the Star Lake Scenic Area.

/// 02

Star Lake Wonderland Lake & Mountain Views

Star Lake is the soul of Qixingyan. The lake surface is like a mirror, with seven peaks reflected upon it, creating a breathtaking tableau of "mountain shadows on the lake, water merging with the sky." Taking a boat tour across Star Lake, weaving between the seven peaks, every turn reveals a different vista.

Qixingyan is not only a natural wonder but also home to millennia-old cliff inscriptions. Inside the Shishi Rock Cave, over 500 cliff inscriptions dating back to the Tang Dynasty have been preserved, known as the "Thousand-Year Poetry Corridor" — the most concentrated collection of cliff inscriptions in Lingnan.

Thousand-Year Poetry Corridor Cliff Inscriptions

Inside the Shishi Rock Cave of Qixingyan, over 500 cliff inscriptions dating from the Tang Dynasty are preserved, earning it the name "Thousand-Year Poetry Corridor." Historical luminaries such as Li Yong, Bao Zheng, and Yu Dayou have left their calligraphy here, making it the most concentrated collection of cliff inscriptions in Lingnan.

The Qixingyan Archway is the iconic landmark of the scenic area, built in 1959, with the three characters "Qixingyan" personally inscribed by Marshal Zhu De. The square in front of the archway is a favorite leisure spot for Zhaoqing residents, brilliantly lit at night, complementing the Star Lake nightscape.
