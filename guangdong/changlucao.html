/// 01

Lingnan Happy Kingdom

Founded in 2000, Chimelong Tourist Resort is located in Lunjiao Sanzhou, Shunde District, Foshan City, adjacent to the main stream of the Pearl River. It is a comprehensive resort rooted in Lingnan history and culture and the charm of Shunde water town, blending large-scale amusement rides, a fairytale animal kingdom, and spectacular stage performances.

Rated as a National 5A Tourist Attraction in 2014, the resort now features eleven major themed parks including Scream Island, Fairytale Animal Kingdom, Mine Cave Aquarium, Military Theme Park, Ice & Snow World, Science Museum, Water World, and more, offering hundreds of rides and attractions.

/// 02

Ultimate Ride Experience

Scream Island brings together hundreds of world-class thrill rides — the "Magic Ring Vertical Roller Coaster" using German MACK technology stands 46.2m high with a top speed of 105 km/h, the "Dragon & Phoenix Coaster" spins on twin tracks like a tornado, and the "Catapult Flyer" launches you 50m into the sky in just 1.8 seconds.

Other highlights include the ghost ship "Göteborg" with advanced sound and light effects, the "Ferris Wheel" offering a panoramic view of the park, and a 5D dynamic cinemascope theater — every visitor can find their own heart-pounding experience.

/// 03

Fairytale Animal Kingdom

The Fairytale Animal Kingdom breaks away from traditional zoo models, using unique garden designs to showcase the mysterious and fascinating side of animals. Areas such as the Bird Paradise, Lion & Tiger Mountain, Giraffe House, Aquarium, and African Savannah allow visitors to get up close with their animal friends.

Its original animal theater features the "Farm Five Wonders" — pig diving, pig racing, duck racing, cockfighting, and pet dog shows, alongside sea lions, black bears, parrots, and other rare animals showing off their talents — a true spectacle of animal wonders.
