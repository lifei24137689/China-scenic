◈ Qinling Canyon · Natural Oxygen BarJinsixia is located southeast of Shangnan County. It is a rare Zhanggu landform geological structure deep in the Qinling Mountains, with sheer canyon walls, cascading waterfalls, and emerald pools. It is known as the "Canyon Capital."

/// 01

Canyon Wonders

Jinsixia is one of China's ten most beautiful canyons, stretching 20.5km in length with a depth of over 10km. The canyon features rugged Zhanggu landforms, meandering streams, and three main gorges — White Dragon Gorge, Black Dragon Gorge, and Azure Dragon Gorge — each with its own unique character.

The canyon walls are steep as if carved by a knife, offering the breathtaking "Thread of Sky" spectacle when looking upward. The scenic area houses over 100 attractions including the Danjiang Source and thirteen-tiered waterfall clusters. Shiyan Stronghold stands atop the cliff, making it the most representative canyon scenic area in the Qinling Mountains.

Flying Waterfalls

Jinsixia boasts thirteen tiers of waterfall clusters with a total drop of nearly 100 meters. The Suolong Waterfall and Shuangxi Waterfall each have their own splendor. The three-tiered waterfalls cascade down like strings of pearls, with the roar of water like thunder and mist drifting like a fairyland.

The streams within the canyon are crystal clear to the bottom, with emerald pools scattered like jade. The Danjiang headwaters emerge from the mountains, with water quality reaching National Class I standards. In summer, the canyon temperature is 5-8°C cooler than outside, making it an ideal summer retreat.

Eco Oxygen Bar

Jinsixia has a forest coverage rate of over 98%, with negative oxygen ion concentrations reaching up to 50,000+ per cubic centimeter. The area is exceptionally rich in biodiversity, with thousands of plant species thriving throughout the canyon.

It is a National Geopark, National Forest Park, and National Water Conservancy Scenic Area. Integrating canyons, waterfalls, stone forests, streams, and karst caves, Jinsixia is a Qinling treasure perfect for leisure, summer retreat, adventure, and scientific exploration.
