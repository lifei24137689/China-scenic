01 · Geographic Wonder

The Beauty of Division

Fenjiezhou Island is located in Lingshui Li Autonomous County, Hainan. Due to its special geographical location, it serves as the climatic dividing line between northern and southern Hainan, hence the name "Fenjiezhou" (Dividing Island). It boasts unique tropical island landforms and abundant marine biological resources, making it one of Hainan's most suitable islands for diving and exploring the underwater world, and is hailed as "The Island That Divides the Soul."

Its unique location creates the peculiar meteorological phenomenon where "it rains at Ox Head while the sun shines at Ox Tail" — on a single island, one can experience two completely different climates.

Dividing Line Natural Wonder Vacation Destination
02 · Ocean Wonderland

Underwater World

Fenjiezhou Island boasts exceptional diving conditions. In its crystal-clear waters, colorful coral reefs and tropical fish abound. The island offers not only marine tourism activities such as diving, sea fishing, and jet skiing, but also features a marine theater where visitors can interact closely with dolphins, sea lions, sea turtles, and other marine animals.

Whether deep-diving to explore the mysterious underwater world or riding a semi-submersible sightseeing boat to admire coral and fish through glass windows, one cannot help but be captivated by this vast blue expanse.

03 · Picturesque Resort

Romantic Island

The island's unique geographical location, climate, and regional culture attract many couples to hold romantic underwater weddings here. Exchanging vows in the blue sea, with colorful fish and coral as witnesses to their love, has established Fenjiezhou Island's distinctive brand of "Romantic Underwater Wedding."

White sandy beaches, azure waters, and swaying coconut groves create a picturesque natural landscape on Fenjiezhou Island. Strolling along the beach, listening to the waves, feeling the sea breeze — this is paradise on earth.
