/// I · Sacred Buddhist Land

The Southernmost Blessed Land in China

Nanshan is the southernmost mountain in China, located in a tropical marine monsoon climate zone with a forest coverage rate of 97%. It features mountain scenery, sea views, and landscaped gardens, integrating Buddhist culture, longevity culture, and ecological culture, renowned as the blessed land of "Sacred Buddhist Land, Longevity like Nanshan".

Walking through Nanshan, you will find ancient towering trees, lush greenery, sea breezes brushing your face, and the sound of Buddhist chants lingering in the air. The Nanshan Cultural Tourism Zone, relying on its unique mountain and sea resources and profound Buddhist cultural heritage, has created a pure land for the soul to return to tranquility.

/// II · Guanyin of the South Sea

The 108m South Sea Guanyin Statue

The 108-meter-tall Guanyin statue stands atop the blue waves of the South Sea, one of the tallest Guanyin statues in the world and an iconic landmark of Sanya. The statue features three aspects in one body — one face holding a sutra, one holding a lotus, and one holding prayer beads, symbolizing wisdom, peace, and compassion.

The Guanyin statue steps on the blue waves of the South Sea, backed by the lush Nanshan mountain, facing the vast ocean, solemn and compassionate. On auspicious Buddhist festivals, devotees from home and abroad gather here to pray and make wishes amidst the lingering Buddhist chants, experiencing the boundless power of the Dharma.

/// III · Zen of Nanshan

A Millennial Zen Sanctuary for the Soul

The Nanshan Cultural Tourism Zone is a large park showcasing traditional Chinese Buddhist culture, featuring numerous Buddhist cultural attractions including Nanshan Temple, the Gate of Non-Duality, and the Thirty-Three Guanyin Hall. Walking through it, the air is filled with Buddhist chants and sandalwood incense, allowing visitors to escape the hustle and bustle and return to inner peace.

The Nanshan Temple complex, built in Tang Dynasty architectural style, is grand and magnificent. The Thirty-Three Guanyin Hall houses various manifestations of Guanyin, while the Gate of Non-Duality symbolizes the true essence of Buddhist wisdom. Each attraction embodies profound Buddhist cultural connotations, waiting for those destined to experience them.
