Rainforest Mystery

Yanoda Tropical Rainforest is Hainan's most valuable tropical rainforest resource museum. Built upon natural landscapes, the scenic area protects and enhances the beautiful natural ecological environment with dense forests, babbling streams, and extremely high concentrations of negative oxygen ions in the air. Known as a "Natural Oxygen Bar," it is an ideal place to escape the urban hustle and return to nature.

🌳 Tropical Rainforest 🌿 Natural Oxygen Bar 🌱 Pristine Ecology
/// 02

Dreamy Three Valleys

The scenic area consists of three major themed landscape zones: Rainforest Valley, Dream Valley, and Sandao Valley. Rainforest Valley showcases the pristine features of tropical rainforests, Dream Valley features waterfalls and streams as its main scenery, while Sandao Valley presents the magnificent canyon landforms. Each of the three valleys has its own unique characteristics, together forming the rich and diverse rainforest landscape of Yanoda.

Rainforest Adventure

Yanoda is not only a sightseeing destination but also an outdoor paradise full of adventure and challenges. The Wahailu Glass Observation Platform lets you overlook the rainforest from above, the Devil Swing and Cliff Sea-view Swing offer an adrenaline rush at the top of the rainforest, and the zipline allows you to fly over the jungle like a bird.
