✦ 01

Li & Miao Homeland

Binglanggu Li & Miao Cultural Tourism Zone consists of seven major cultural experience areas including the Intangible Cultural Heritage Village, Ganshen Li Village, Guyin Miao Village, and Field Li Village, offering beautiful scenery and unique resources. It is one of the main venues for the traditional Li & Miao "Third of March" and "Qixi Water Festival," and is renowned as a living museum of Hainan's ethnic culture.

Walking into Binglanggu is like traveling through a time tunnel — every village and every boat-shaped house tells the wisdom and stories of the Li and Miao ancestors. Amidst the dense areca palm forests, ancient Li and Miao villages are scattered in an orderly fashion, creating a moving picture of ethnic customs.

✦ 02

Intangible Heritage Treasures

The scenic area showcases ten national-level intangible cultural heritage items, among which the "Traditional Li Spinning, Dyeing, Weaving and Embroidery Techniques" have been inscribed on UNESCO's List of Intangible Cultural Heritage in Need of Urgent Safeguarding. Here, you can witness the exquisite craftsmanship of Li brocade weaving and experience the inheritance and charm of millennia-old ethnic culture.

Li brocade is regarded as the "oracle bone script" of the Li people — its patterns and motifs record the Li people's understanding of nature, deities and life. At Binglanggu, inheritors demonstrate the entire process of spinning, dyeing, weaving and embroidery by hand, as colorful threads dance between their fingers, weaving breathtaking chapters of Li brocade.

✦ 04

Ancient Charm, Modern Spirit

The large-scale live performance Binglang · Ancient Rhyme uses the landscape as its stage and Li & Miao culture as its soul, vividly recreating the production, daily life scenes and folk customs of the Li and Miao people. The scenic area also features modern recreational facilities such as field go-karts, amphibious off-road vehicles, and an aerial mini-train, where tradition and modernity blend perfectly.

As ancient Li and Miao songs echo through the areca palm forests and modern laughter soars over the fields, Binglanggu attracts every visitor with its unique charm. This is not just a cultural museum, but a living space where tradition and modernity resonate in harmony.
