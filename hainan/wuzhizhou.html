PARADISE BAY

Lover's Paradise

Nestled within Haitang Bay on the northern coast of Sanya, Wuzhizhou Island is one of the few islands around Hainan with abundant freshwater resources and lush vegetation. Blessed with exceptional diving conditions and a full range of sea, land, and air water activities, it is renowned as the "Maldives of China" — an island resort destination that integrates marine recreation, signature diving experiences, and premium hotels.

DIVING HEAVEN

Diving Paradise

Wuzhizhou Island is acclaimed as "China's Premier Diving Base" and is one of the few islands in the world free of rocks or pebbles. With crystal-clear waters, dazzling coral reefs, and an abundance of marine life, it presents a dreamlike underwater world for diving enthusiasts.

ISLAND CARNIVAL

Island Carnival

The island offers over 30 sea and land activities including diving, jet skiing, parasailing, banana boats, and flying fish — creating a unique four-dimensional experience spanning "underwater, surface, air, and land". With pleasant weather year-round, it is an ideal destination for vacations, leisure, winter escapes, swimming, and entertainment.
