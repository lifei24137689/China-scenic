End of the Sky Corner of the Sea

Tianya Haijiao Scenic Area is located 23 km southwest of Sanya, backed by Maling Mountain and facing the vast sea. Here, azure waters merge with the blue sky, misty waves stretch far and wide, sailboats dot the horizon, coconut palms sway in the breeze, and exotic rocks stand in abundance. Massive boulders inscribed with "Tianya", "Haijiao", and "Southern Sky Pillar" rise from the beach, making it the most iconic cultural landmark in Hainan.

Southern Sky Pillar Natural Wonder

The most famous attractions in the scenic area are the massive boulders inscribed with "Tianya", "Haijiao", and "Southern Sky Pillar". These stone carvings are not only a perfect fusion of nature and culture but also carry the boundless imagination of scholars and poets throughout the centuries. The "Southern Sky Pillar" was once featured on the back of the 2-yuan RMB banknote, making it a household name.

Romantic Tianya

Tianya Haijiao is renowned both at home and abroad for its captivating tropical coastal scenery and its unique, time-honored Tianya culture. In 2024, it was officially accredited as a national 5A-level tourist attraction, becoming another golden name card for Hainan tourism. It is also a popular destination for wedding photography and romantic dates, allowing every couple to make eternal vows at the ends of the earth.
