Cliff Plank Walkway

Shenxianju is located in Xianju County, Taizhou City — a national 5A scenic area renowned for its extraordinary peaks, bizarre rocks, plunging waterfalls, and secluded valleys. It is known as "a land where immortals dwell." The cliff plank walkways snake along the precipices, making visitors feel as if they are walking above the clouds.

Goddess Peak, General's Rock, Sleeping Beauty, and other remarkable rock formations appear vividly lifelike. Flying Waterfall cascades from a hundred meters high, shrouding the area in mist with rainbows frequently appearing. Ruyi Bridge spans between two peaks, standing as the iconic landmark of Shenxianju.

/// 02

Ruyi Bridge

Ruyi Bridge is one of the most breathtaking attractions in Shenxianju. This suspension bridge stretches between two peaks, with its deck suspended hundreds of meters above the valley floor. Standing on the bridge, you look down into a bottomless abyss while surrounded by misty peaks — as if stepping into a celestial realm.

Geologically, Shenxianju was formed by Cretaceous volcanic eruptions. Its rhyolite terrain has created a unique landscape of clustered peaks. Goddess Peak towers into the clouds, General's Rock stands in majestic grandeur, and Sleeping Beauty lies in serene repose — each a masterpiece of nature's craftsmanship.

Flying Waterfall

The Flying Waterfall is the most spectacular cascade in Shenxianju, plunging from a hundred-meter-high cliff with a thunderous roar and overwhelming force. Sunlight streaming through the mist often conjures rainbows, adding an air of mystery to this already enchanted landscape.

Shenxianju is beautiful in every season — wildflowers bloom in spring, clouds and mist swirl in summer, forests blaze with autumn colors, and snow cloaks the peaks in winter. Whether hiking the trails or riding the cable car, visitors can fully appreciate the unique charm of this "land where immortals dwell."
