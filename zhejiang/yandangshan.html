/// 01

A Celebrated Mountain by the Sea

Yandangshan, located in Wenzhou, Zhejiang, is world-renowned for its extraordinary mountain and water scenery, hailed as a "celebrated mountain by the sea, unsurpassed wonder under heaven." Formed by volcanic eruptions 120 million years ago, it is the most representative Cretaceous rhyolite caldera along the volcanic belt of the circum-Pacific continental margin.

Lingfeng, Lingyan, and Dalongqiu are known as the "Three Wonders of Yandang." The night scenery of Lingfeng is a unique spectacle, with peaks transforming into various fantastical shapes under the moonlight. The cliff-dangling performance at Lingyan is thrilling, and Dalongqiu Waterfall, with a drop of 197 meters, is one of the tallest waterfalls in China.

/// 02

Three Wonders of Yandang

Lingfeng is the eastern gateway of Yandangshan, famous for its peculiar peaks and grotesque rock scenery by night. Hezhang Peak resembles hands clasped in prayer, with Guanyin Cave hidden within its crevices, featuring a nine-story pavilion built into the cave. As night falls, the silhouettes of the peaks shift into mesmerizing forms — lovers embracing, eagles spreading their wings — leaving visitors in awe.

Lingyan Scenic Area centers around Lingyan Temple, with Pingxiazhang standing like a brocade screen. The high-altitude wire walk between Tianzhu Peak and Zhanqi Peak is a breathtaking performance. Xiaolongqiu Waterfall plunges from a height of hundreds of meters, filling the air with mist and occasional rainbows.

/// 03

Geological Wonder

Yandangshan is a UNESCO Global Geopark, with its rhyolite landforms holding immense scientific and scenic value. The Fangdong Scenic Area perches on sheer cliffs hundreds of meters high, with plank roads hanging like heavenly ladders, making visitors feel as if walking among the clouds. Sanzhe Waterfall cascades in three distinct tiers, each with its own unique character.

Yandangshan is also a mountain of culture, where generations of literati have left behind numerous cliffside inscriptions and poems. Famous historical figures such as Xie Lingyun, Shen Kuo, and Xu Xiake all visited Yandangshan. Xu Xiake made three journeys here, leaving behind the praise: "Sharp peaks and layered cliffs, a myriad of exquisite wonders."
