/// 01

Three Stone Pillars

Located in Jiangshan City, Quzhou, Jianglangshan is world-famous for its "Three Stone Pillars" — three colossal stone peaks rising abruptly from the ground as if carved by knives and axes. It is a classic representative of China's Danxia landform and is inscribed on the World Natural Heritage list.

The "Line of Sky" is the most thrilling sight at Jianglangshan — a narrow crevice between two stone peaks that only allows one person to pass, with only a sliver of blue sky visible overhead. Xu Xiake visited Jianglangshan three times and marveled that "among all the famous mountains I have explored, Jianglangshan stands above all."

/// 02

Nianba Du

Nianbadu Ancient Town, located at the southwestern tip of Jiangshan City at the junction of Zhejiang, Fujian, and Jiangxi provinces, was an important post station on the ancient Xianxia Road. The town preserves a wealth of Ming and Qing dynasty architecture — blue stone paths, horse-head walls, and carved windows and doors, all exuding an antique charm.

The name "Nianbadu" originates from historical military garrisons. The town is home to a remarkable diversity of surnames and dialects, earning it the reputation of a "Kingdom of Dialects." Ancient structures such as Wenchang Hall and Guandi Temple are well-preserved, serving as living fossils for the study of Ming-Qing trade culture and migration history.

Xianxia Ancient Path

Xianxia Pass was a vital strategic gateway between ancient Zhejiang and Fujian. The Xianxia Ancient Path snakes through steep mountains and was an essential route for merchants and travelers in bygone eras. Fugai Mountain features rugged rock formations and deep caves, making it a paradise for adventure enthusiasts.

The combination of Jianglangshan and Nianbadu offers both the wonders of nature and the richness of human history. Climb Jianglangshan to witness the Danxia spectacle, wander through Nianbadu to savor its ancient town charm, and hike Xianxia Pass to trace the legacy of the old road — three distinct experiences in one journey.
