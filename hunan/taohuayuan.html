/// Chapter I

Peach Blossom Land

Taohuayuan is the prototypical site of Tao Yuanming's timeless masterpiece "The Story of the Peach Blossom Spring." For centuries, countless scholars and recluses have journeyed here in search of the idyllic paradise of their imagination. The poetic vision of "fresh grass and falling petals in profusion" transforms into a tangible landscape before your eyes.

The scenic area centers on Qin Stream and Qin Valley, where winding waters and deep mountain valleys recreate the pastoral scene from "The Story of the Peach Blossom Spring" — "where the peach forest ended at the water source, and there stood a mountain with a small opening that seemed to let in light." Stepping here feels like traveling back a thousand years into the ideal world penned by Tao Yuanming.

/// Chapter II

Pastoral Poetry

Peach Blossom Mountain is lush with verdant trees and meandering paths, a scenic retreat where generations of literati have climbed to compose poetry. Taoyuan Mountain features layered peaks and babbling streams, offering distinct beauty across all four seasons — peach blossoms blanketing the hills in spring, verdant canopies in summer, vibrant autumn hues, and pristine white snow in winter.

Deep in Qin Valley, crisscross paths connect the fields, the sounds of roosters and dogs echo between farmhouses, and smoke curls from kitchen chimneys — a living tableau of pastoral life from a thousand years ago. Wandering through it feels like stepping into a flowing ink-wash landscape painting, where every step is a poem and every glance a picture.

/// Chapter III

Heaven on Earth

From March to April each year, Taohuayuan enters its most beautiful season — thousands of acres of peach blossoms burst into bloom like pink clouds and rosy mists, blanketing the hills and valleys. The sea of pink flowers, set against lush green mountains and clear waters, creates an exquisitely romantic spring panorama, drawing countless visitors to this peach blossom rendezvous.

As the core cultural IP of Tao Yuanming's legacy, Taohuayuan is not only a gift of nature but also a spiritual landmark of Chinese reclusive culture and pastoral poetry. Whether you are following in the footsteps of literary heritage or seeking inner peace, you will find your own "Peach Blossom Land" here.
