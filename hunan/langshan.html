/// 01

Danxia Soul

Mount Langshan is a vital part of the China Danxia World Natural Heritage site, renowned for its mature Danxia peak cluster and peak forest landforms. Legend has it that in ancient times, Emperor Shun toured the south and, upon seeing the beautiful landscape, created the character "崀" to name it, meaning "the finest of mountains."

The Danxia landform here developed in its mature stage, featuring diverse forms such as peak clusters, peak forests, canyons, and valley gorges. The red sandstone glistens brilliantly under the sunlight, like flames burning on the earth, showcasing the most magnificent aspect of Danxia landforms.

/// 02

Fantastic Peaks & Rocks

Chili Peak is the most iconic landmark of Mount Langshan. This giant stone pillar, 180 meters tall, is wider at the top and narrower at the bottom, resembling an upside-down chili pepper, earning it the title of "The World's Number One Chili Pepper." It is a quintessential example of Danxia landform in its late development stage, a breathtaking sight to behold.

Bajiao Village towers atop Mount Langshan, offering a panoramic view of both Hunan and Guangxi provinces. Camel Peak bears a striking resemblance to a desert ship striding forward with its head held high. Tianyi Alley is a narrow crevice canyon, where the tightest section allows only one person to pass through sideways.

/// 03

Purple Haze Secret Realm

Purple Haze Cave is a serene valley in Mount Langshan, named for the purple-hued mist that often envelops it at dawn and dusk. With dense vegetation and babbling streams, it stands in stark contrast to the surrounding majestic Danxia peak forests, showcasing the softer and more graceful side of Mount Langshan.

Legend has it that Emperor Shun once paused here during his southern tour and was deeply moved by the beauty, inspiring him to create the character "崀" in commemoration. For thousands of years, the landscape and culture of Langshan have intertwined and coexisted, forming a unique mountain culture — a dual treasure of nature and humanity.
