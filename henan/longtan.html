Zhanggu Wonder

Red Rock Zhanggu Wonder

Longtan Grand Canyon is the core scenic area of Daimei Mountain World Geopark, renowned for its typical red rock zhanggu (barrier gorge) landforms. Stretching 12 kilometers in length, the canyon features a series of barrier gorges and narrow gorges distributed like a string of beads, earning it the title of "China's Premier Zhanggu Canyon."

Over hundreds of millions of years, geological movements have shaped the red sandstone into spectacular gorge clusters, with towering cliffs standing thousands of feet tall — a magnificent epic of Earth's evolution.

Waterfall Cluster

Seven Pools & Waterfalls

Longtan Grand Canyon boasts seven major pools and waterfall clusters, where spring water cascades down red rock cliffs with thunderous roar. The pools are crystal clear and emerald green, forming a stunning contrast with the red rocks — a magnificent landscape painting.

Streams gurgle and waterfalls cascade through the canyon, with a pool every few steps and a waterfall every ten paces, creating a unique canyon waterscape system.

  • Longtan Waterfall — the canyon's most spectacular waterfall
  • Green Dragon Pool — a jade-like deep pool
  • Black Dragon Pool — a mysterious and deep canyon pool
  • Five Dragon Pool Waterfall Cluster — five-tier cascading waterfalls
