/// 01

Summit of Central Plains · Yuhuang Peak

Yuhuang Peak, the main summit of Baiyun Mountain, stands at 2,216 meters above sea level — the highest peak in the Central Plains region. From the top, the view opens up to rolling green mountains and surging seas of clouds, as if stepping into a fairyland. Named after the Jade Emperor Pavilion built on its summit, Yuhuang Peak has been a sacred site for Taoist pilgrimage since ancient times.

Baiyun Mountain is a UNESCO World Geopark, nestled deep in the Funiu Mountain range. The terrain is majestic and steep, celebrated for its abundant and spectacular cloud formations. The Funiu Mountains stretch across western Henan, with Baiyun Mountain as their crown jewel and the geographical apex of the Central China plain.

/// 02

Rolling Sea of Clouds · Finest Waterfall in Central Plains

Baiyun Mountain is famed for its abundant and wondrous clouds. With ample moisture in the air, the peaks are shrouded in mist year-round. After a rainfall clears, seas of clouds surge from the valleys, cresting over the summits to create a breathtaking spectacle. Standing atop Yuhuang Peak, with waves of clouds churning beneath and mist swirling around, one feels transported to a celestial realm.

Jiulong Waterfall plunges 103 meters, making it one of the most famous waterfalls in Central China. The water roars down the cliff, sending up clouds of mist. When sunlight breaks through the spray, rainbows often arch across the valley, adding a touch of grace to the rugged grandeur of the mountains.

/// 03

Natural Oxygen Bar · Forest Hidden Realm

With a forest coverage rate of 98.5%, Baiyun Mountain is a true natural forest oxygen bar. In the dense primeval woods, the concentration of negative oxygen ions is exceptionally high, making every breath refreshing and invigorating. Walking through the forest, you are surrounded by towering ancient trees, twisting vines, babbling streams, and melodious birdsong.

Luhua Valley is a serene valley within Baiyun Mountain. In late summer and early autumn, the valley is covered with flying reeds like snow, creating a picturesque scene against the green hills and clear waters. It is an ideal summer retreat, with an average temperature of only 24°C, earning it the reputation of being the "Central Plains Summer Paradise."
