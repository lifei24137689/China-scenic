/// 01 Millennium Scroll

Millennium Scroll

Qingming Riverside Landscape Garden is a large-scale Song Dynasty historical and cultural theme park, reconstructed based on the renowned painting "Along the River During the Qingming Festival" by Northern Song artist Zhang Zeduan. The scroll depicts the bustling market scenes of the Northern Song capital Bianjing (today's Kaifeng) during the Qingming Festival, and this theme park brings every pavilion, tower, riverside market, and street scene from the painting to life.

Following the layout of the original scroll, the park masterfully recreates the vibrant atmosphere of the Northern Song capital. Visitors strolling through the park feel as if they have traveled a thousand years back in time, stepping into an era of commercial prosperity and cultural brilliance. Every step reveals a scene straight out of the painting.

/// 02 Song Dynasty Splendor

Song Dynasty Splendor

Stepping into Qingming Riverside Garden, you are greeted by magnificent Song Dynasty-style architecture. The towering city gate Shangshan Gate stands in grandeur, while the Rainbow Bridge spans across the Bian River, recreating the bustling scenes along its banks as depicted in the scroll. Fuyun Pavilion rises high into the clouds; climbing to its top offers a panoramic view of the entire park.

The park recreates every aspect of Song Dynasty daily life: taverns and tea houses, general stores, handcraft workshops, and performers dressed in Song attire everywhere acrobats, merchants, and street performers. It feels as though you have stepped onto the streets of Bianliang a thousand years ago, experiencing the elegance and prosperity of the Great Song Dynasty.

/// 03 Dongjing Dream

Dongjing Dream

"Dongjing Dream of the Great Song Dynasty" is the park's most representative outdoor water-stage performance. Set against the backdrop of Northern Song history and culture, it uses modern sound, light, and electrical technology to present a magnificent visual spectacle on the lake. The performance recreates the court ceremonies, market life, battle scenes, and cultural celebrations of the Song Dynasty at its peak.

In addition, the park offers a rich variety of Song Dynasty folk performances: cuju (ancient Chinese soccer), cockfighting, shadow puppetry, acrobatics, puppet shows, and other traditional arts rotate throughout the day. During traditional festivals, grand lantern fairs and temple fairs are held, allowing visitors to experience the unique charm of Song culture in a joyful and festive atmosphere.
