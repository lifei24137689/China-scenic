Epicenter · Rebirth

Wenchuan Special Tourism Zone is located in Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province, and stands as a model of post-disaster reconstruction. The scenic area includes core regions such as Yingxiu Town, Shuimo Ancient Town, and Sanjiang Ecological Tourism Zone, covering a total area of approximately 50 square kilometers.

Yingxiu Town was the epicenter of the 5·12 Great Earthquake. The Xuankou Middle School site preserves the original appearance of the earthquake's moment, serving as an important memorial to remember history and cherish life. Today, Yingxiu has risen from the ruins, showcasing tenacious vitality and the miracle of reconstruction.

Shuimo · Ancient Town

Shuimo Ancient Town blends Tibetan-Qiang culture with Zen culture, earning the reputation of "Ink-Wash Paradise." Nestled between mountains and rivers, ancient buildings wind along the river valley. Blue stone paths, wooden stilt houses, and old water mills exude an air of timeless charm.

Chanshou Old Street is the soul of Shuimo Ancient Town, preserving a large number of Ming and Qing dynasty buildings. Tea houses, inns, and handicraft workshops line the street in abundance. Here, you can savor a cup of Tibetan tea, listen to the sound of Qiang flutes, and experience the slow-paced life of an ancient town.

Sanjiang · Ecology

Sanjiang Ecological Tourism Zone is located in the southern part of Wenchuan County, named for the confluence of the Xi River, Zhong River, and Heishi River. With layered green mountains, babbling streams, and extremely high forest coverage, it is a natural oxygen bar. Rich in rare flora and fauna, it is home to protected species such as giant pandas and golden snub-nosed monkeys.

Strolling through the primeval forests of Sanjiang Ecological Zone, you will find towering ancient trees draped with vines, cascading waterfalls, and birds singing in the serene mountains. The area features multiple hiking trails suitable for visitors of different fitness levels, allowing everyone to embrace nature and appreciate the beauty of the landscape.
