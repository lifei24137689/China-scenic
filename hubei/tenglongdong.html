Overview

Located in Lichuan City, Enshi Prefecture, Hubei Province, Tenglongdong is the largest explored cave in China and one of the world's extraordinary caves. With a constant internal temperature of 12–18°C, it integrates mountains, water, caves, and forests, renowned for its grandeur, peril, wonder, seclusion, and beauty.
