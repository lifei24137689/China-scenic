Oriental Colorado · Nature's Masterpiece of Sinkholes and Canyons

Enshi Grand Canyon is renowned for its geological wonders including sinkholes, crevices, sheer cliffs, and peak clusters. Yunlong Crevice, known as "the most beautiful scar on Earth," stretches 7.5 kilometers and reaches a depth of 75 meters. Walking through it, with precipitous cliffs on both sides and babbling streams below, the scenery is truly breathtaking.

A Stick of Incense · A Geological Wonder Standing for Millennia

A Stick of Incense is the iconic landmark of Enshi Grand Canyon — a 150-meter-tall rock pillar that has stood for tens of thousands of years. This "stone pillar" is wider at the top and narrower at the bottom, with a minimum diameter of just 4 meters. It has weathered billions of years of wind and rain, standing as a world-class geological wonder.

Precipice Corridor · A Thrilling Journey Above the Clouds

The Precipice Corridor is built on a 1,700-meter-high cliff, stretching approximately 500 meters. Walking along this cliffside plank road, with a bottomless abyss beneath your feet and the rolling Wuling Mountains in the distance, surrounded by swirling clouds and mist, you feel as if you have stepped into a fairyland.
