800-li Qingjiang · Mother River of the Tujia People

The 800-li Qingjiang River is as beautiful as a painting, with the 300-li gallery stretching through Changyang. Qingjiang is the mother river of the Tujia people, with emerald-green waters and verdant mountains on both sides, like an unfolding landscape scroll. Taking a boat tour on the Qingjiang feels like traveling through a painting.

The Qingjiang River originates in Lichuan, Hubei, flowing through Enshi, Jianshi, Badong, and Changyang before joining the Yangtze River at Yidu. Along the way, picturesque landscapes blend mountains and waters, earning it the reputation of "the Danube of the East".

Inverted Shadow Gorge · A Dreamy World of Water Reflections

Inverted Shadow Gorge is the most spectacular section of the Qingjiang Gallery. The verdant mountains on both banks are mirrored in the emerald river, blending reality and illusion. In the early morning, mist veils the river surface, creating hazy reflections that feel like a poetic fairyland.

Stretching about 5 kilometers, Inverted Shadow Gorge features towering peaks and dense vegetation along its banks. The river water here is so clear that you can see straight to the bottom, creating a unique "water mirror" landscape. When a gentle breeze ripples the surface, the reflections sway gracefully — an endlessly beautiful sight.

Birthplace of the Ba People · Spiritual Home of Tujia Culture

Wuluo Zhongli Mountain is the birthplace of Linjun, the ancestor of the Ba people, and a cultural sacred site for the Tujia ethnic group. Temples such as the Xiangwang Temple and White Tiger Hall stand here, drawing Tujia descendants from far and wide for ancestral pilgrimage each year.

Intangible cultural heritages such as Tujia songs and dances and Nangu music are preserved here. The Tujia people have their own unique language, costumes, and customs. Traditional dances like the Hand-waving Dance and Sayerhe are passionate and exuberant, while the exquisite Tujia brocade "Xilankapu" showcases the profound richness of this ethnic culture.
