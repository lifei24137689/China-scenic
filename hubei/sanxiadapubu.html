Top Ten Waterfalls of China · Majestic Waterfall Spectacle

Three Gorges Waterfall, formerly known as Baiguoshu Waterfall, stands 102m tall and 80m wide, making it one of China's top ten waterfalls. It cascades down like a white silk ribbon, with thunderous sounds and majestic momentum. During the rainy season, the water volume surges dramatically, audible from miles away.

Walk-Through Experience · Feel the Thrill Up Close

The most unique experience at Three Gorges Waterfall is the "Walk-Through." A dedicated boardwalk allows visitors to pass behind the cascading water, feeling the immense torrents roar overhead. In summer, it offers a refreshing and exhilarating adventure.

The scenic area also features karst cave attractions such as Golden Lion Cave and Lover's Spring. Golden Lion Cave stretches approximately 1,800 meters, filled with stunning stalactites, stalagmites, curtains, and flowers in myriad shapes, creating a dreamlike ambiance under colorful lights.
