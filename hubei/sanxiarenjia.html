Gorge Customs · A Paradise in Xiling Gorge

Three Gorges Folk Village is located along the Xiling Gorge section of the Yangtze River, nestled against mountains and waters in a breathtaking landscape. Longjin Creek shimmers with emerald water, stilted houses line both banks in scattered harmony, and canopy boats drift through — bringing to life the poetic vision of "the most beautiful scene in the Three Gorges is its people."

/// 02

Ba-Chu Culture · A Living Museum of Millennia

Three Gorges Folk Village embodies the essence of Ba-Chu culture and Tujia folk traditions. Bawang Stockade stands atop the mountain, recreating the ancient Ba people's defensive fortifications. The folk songs of Tujia maidens echo through the canyons, while the sound of ancient looms weaves stories of time.

Life on the Water · Poetic Dwelling by the Gorge

The waterborne community at Three Gorges Folk Village is its most captivating sight. Fishing boats cast nets, white sails dot the river, and fishermen live and work on the water, carrying on a millennia-old gorge lifestyle. Riverside villagers wash clothes and prepare meals by the creek, with cooking smoke rising like a scene from a painting.
