Hometown of Hua Mulan Spiritual Home of a Legendary Heroine

The Mulan Cultural Eco-tourism Area is themed around the hometown of Hua Mulan, encompassing four major scenic areas: Mulan Mountain, Mulan Tianchi, Mulan Grassland, and Mulan Cloud Mist Mountain. Mulan Mountain is the hometown of General Mulan, where Buddhism and Taoism coexist on the same mountain, rich in cultural heritage.

The story of Hua Mulan joining the army in place of her father has been told for millennia. Every plant and tree here carries reverence for this legendary heroine. The four scenic areas each have their own character, where natural beauty and cultural landscapes complement each other perfectly.

Mulan Tianchi Azure Wonderland on the Heights

Mulan Tianchi is located at the highest point of Mulan Mountain, a high-altitude lake. Its waters are clear as a mirror, surrounded by lush forests and rolling mountains. Strolling around the lake, you'll find blue waves rippling, birds singing, and flowers blooming — a true paradise on earth.

The crystal-clear waters reflect the blue sky, white clouds, and green mountains. Taking a boat ride, with a gentle breeze on your face, shimmering water, and misty mountain views, brings pure relaxation. This is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Mulan Grassland Central China's Only Prairie Experience

Mulan Grassland is the only prairie-style scenic area in Central China. On the vast grasslands, horses gallop and herds of cattle and sheep roam. Visitors can ride horses, practice archery, camp, and barbecue, experiencing the bold and unconstrained life of the prairie. The evening bonfire parties are especially enchanting.

The grassland stretches endlessly, verdant grass dotted with colorful wildflowers. Under the blue sky and white clouds, yurts are scattered across the landscape, as if transporting you to the northern grasslands. Here, General Mulan's heroic spirit is perfectly presented amidst the southern landscape.
