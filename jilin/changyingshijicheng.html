Jilin's Oriental Hollywood

Located in the Jingyuetan Tourism Economic Development Zone of Changchun, Changying Century City is built upon the rich heritage of Changchun Film Studio. It is China's first world-class movie theme park, renowned as the "Oriental Hollywood."

Covering approximately 1 square kilometer, the scenic area centers on cinematic cultural experiences, integrating over a dozen movie special-effects attractions such as laser-floating films, dynamic dome-screen movies, and 3D giant-screen theaters. It opened in 2005 and was rated a 5A scenic spot in 2015.

A Spectacle of Movie Effects

The park's core attractions include 4D effects cinemas, laser-floating theaters, giant-screen theaters, 5D cinemas, and other high-tech movie experience facilities. Interactive projects like Space Expedition, Speed Shuttle, and Hero Showground allow visitors to immerse themselves in the magic of cinema.

The park is divided into themed zones including the Innovative Technology Experience Area, Thrilling Movie Experience Area, and Dreamy Performance Area. The annual Summer Film Culture Festival draws large crowds, sharing the charm of Chinese film culture with every visitor.

Screen Magic at Your Fingertips

In the Space Expedition project, visitors board a space capsule for a breathtaking journey across the galaxy; at the Speed Shuttle, feel the rush of velocity and adrenaline; at the Film Stunt Museum, discover the behind-the-scenes secrets of moviemaking. Every attraction makes you feel as if you have stepped into the world of cinema.

The park also features a Children's Film Experience Zone and a Film Culture Museum, showcasing the evolution of China's film industry from classic old movies to modern blockbuster spectacles — an unmissable dreamland for movie lovers.
