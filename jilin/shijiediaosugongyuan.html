Five Halls Five Squares

The park features a layout of "five halls and five squares." The five halls include Changchun Sculpture Art Museum, Songshan Hanrong African Art Collection Museum, Wei Xiaoming Art Museum, Changchun Sculpture Museum, and the Sculpture Experience Hall.

Rodin Square houses three masterpieces by the great artist: "The Thinker," "The Age of Bronze," and "Balzac," all cast from Rodin's original molds. The centerpiece of Spring Square, the "Friendship, Peace, Spring" sculpture, stands 29.5 meters tall and is assembled from 164 pieces of granite.

Rodin's Light of Thought

In 2008, after three years of effort, the Changchun city government navigated numerous hurdles including the European Commission and the French Ministry of Culture to successfully bring in an original mold cast of Rodin's "The Thinker." This world-class art treasure, equivalent to the original, became the park's crown jewel.

Centered around Moore Lake, sculptures from various countries line the lakeside walkways. "Extension of Peace" (by Mark Whiteback, USA) stands in Peace Square, its two curved panels forming a gateway shape that symbolizes artistic dialogue across borders.
