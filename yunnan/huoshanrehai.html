Fiery Pulse from the Deep

Located in Tengchong, Baoshan, Yunnan, the Volcano & Hot Springs scenic area is world-famous for its volcanic terrain and geothermal springs. As one of China's three major geothermal regions, 97 volcanoes arranged in a linear pattern form a living "Volcanic Geological Wonderbook."

The Hot Springs area features over 80 steam vents and hot spring clusters, with 10 reaching temperatures above 90°C. The most famous Boiling Pool churns day and night, like the heartbeat of the earth — one of Asia's most spectacular geothermal sights.

A Sea of Springs Cleanses the Soul

The Hot Springs area is China's densest cluster of geothermal sights — Toad's Mouth, Pearl Spring, Glasses Spring, Beauty Pool... each named for its unique shape. The mineral-rich waters are believed to have excellent therapeutic benefits.

Ming Dynasty travel writer Xu Xiake once visited here, documenting the wonders of the Hot Springs in his travelogue. Today, the area features well-developed resort facilities, allowing every visitor to experience the healing power of "a sea of hot springs."
