/// 01

Vast Desert Long River Sunset

Located in Zhongwei City, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Shapotou sits on the southeastern edge of the Tengger Desert. The Yellow River cuts through the desert, creating the magnificent vista of "a lonely smoke straight in the vast desert, the setting sun round over the long river." This unique landscape brings together desert, Yellow River, mountains, and oases, presenting a majestic northwestern panorama.

When Tang Dynasty poet Wang Wei passed through here on an imperial mission to the Hexi frontier, he was so moved by the vast desert and long river that he wrote the timeless poem "On a Mission to the Frontier." Today, Shapotou has developed into a national 5A-level scenic area, receiving over one million visitors annually and becoming a golden landmark of Ningxia tourism.

/// 02

Yellow River Rafting Millennium Raft Song

The goatskin raft is an ancient river-crossing tool of the upper Yellow River, with a history of over a thousand years. At Shapotou, riding a goatskin raft down the Yellow River is the most distinctive cultural experience. The raftsmen sing northwestern folk songs in deep, resonant voices as the raft rises and falls on the mighty Yellow River, with desert scenery stretching along both banks.

Each goatskin raft is made from dozens of inflated goatskin bags tied together — lightweight yet tough. This ancient watercraft embodies the wisdom of the people living along the Yellow River in their struggle against nature. Today, it has become Shapotou's most iconic cultural tourism attraction, drawing countless visitors from home and abroad.

/// 03

Sand Surfing Thrilling Flight

Shapotou boasts China's largest natural sand-sledding slope, standing about a hundred meters high with a steep gradient. Racing down from the top, the wind whistles past your ears and sand grains fly, delivering unprecedented speed and excitement. At the foot of the slope lies the surging Yellow River, and the rush downhill feels as if you are about to plunge into the river, making your heart race.

Beyond sand sledding, activities such as desert surfing, camel trekking across the Tengger Desert, and desert go-karting are equally thrilling. Standing atop the sand dune, you can gaze at rolling sand dunes on one side and the meandering Yellow River on the other — the desert and the long river converge here, creating a breathtaking panorama.
