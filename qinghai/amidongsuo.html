Heavenly Qilian

Amidongsuo, meaning "Thousand Soldiers' Outpost" in Tibetan and commonly known as Niuxin Mountain, is located in Babao Town, Qilian County, Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Qinghai Province. It is a landmark peak of the Qilian Mountains. At 4,667 meters above sea level, the mountain showcases all four seasons at once — snow-capped summit, lush pastures on the mountainside, verdant forests at the foothills, and golden farmland below. It is renowned as the "Little Switzerland of the East."

The mountain is composed of granite, standing tall and majestic, facing Babao Town across the river. As a symbol of Qilian, Amidongsuo embodies the reverence and worship of the local Tibetan herders for this sacred mountain. Every year, numerous pilgrims come to circle the mountain and pray for blessings.

Vertical Ecosystem

The vertical ecological distribution of Amidongsuo is a spectacle. Above 3,500 meters lies the alpine meadow zone, where summer brings lush green grass, wildflowers everywhere, and yaks and Tibetan sheep scattered across the landscape. Below 3,500 meters is the coniferous forest zone, with Qinghai spruce and Qilian juniper standing tall and sturdy, mountain streams flowing through the woods, and the air crisp and clean.

Covering a total area of 112.97 square kilometers with high forest coverage, the scenic area is an important part of Qilian Mountain National Park. It is home to snow leopards, blue sheep, red deer, and many other rare wildlife species, serving as a natural gene bank of plateau biodiversity.

Cultural Sanctuary

Amidongsuo is not only a natural wonder but also a cultural sanctuary. Local Tibetan people worship Amidongsuo as a sacred mountain. Every year on the 15th day of the sixth lunar month, a grand mountain worship ceremony is held, featuring incense burning, prayer flag hanging, and wind horse scattering, to pray for favorable weather and prosperity for people and livestock. Babao Town at the foot of the mountain serves as the political and cultural center of Qilian County, where multiple ethnic groups live in harmony.

In 2020, Amidongsuo Scenic Area was officially rated as a National 5A Tourist Attraction, becoming another heavyweight tourism landmark in Qinghai Province. The supporting facilities are increasingly well-developed. Visitors can drive themselves or take sightseeing buses to explore deep into the area and experience the grandeur and tranquility of the Qilian Mountain hinterland.
