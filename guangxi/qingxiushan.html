Nanning's Green Lung

Qingxiu Mountain Scenic Area is located in the southeastern part of Nanning's city center. As a national 5A-level tourist attraction, it is renowned as the "Green Lung of Nanning." Covering approximately 13 square kilometers with a forest coverage rate of over 98%, it is the largest natural oxygen bar and urban forest park within Nanning.

Named for its elegant peaks and lush greenery, Qingxiu Mountain has been a celebrated scenic spot in Nanning since ancient times. With dense vegetation, fresh air, and extremely high levels of negative oxygen ions, it is an ideal destination for locals seeking leisure and fitness, as well as for visitors exploring nature.

Dragon-Elephant Ancient Pagoda

The Dragon-Elephant Pagoda is the iconic landmark of Qingxiu Mountain. Originally built during the Wanli reign of the Ming Dynasty (1618 AD), this nine-story pagoda stands approximately 52 meters tall, making it the tallest ancient pagoda in Nanning. With an octagonal shape, brick-and-wood structure, and soaring eaves, it exudes timeless solemnity and grandeur. Climbing to the top offers a panoramic view of the entire city of Nanning and the beautiful Yong River.

The name "Dragon-Elephant" is derived from the Buddhist scripture's "power of the dragon and elephant," symbolizing auspiciousness. Throughout history, the pagoda was damaged and rebuilt several times. The current Dragon-Elephant Pagoda was reconstructed in recent years based on its original Ming Dynasty appearance, making it one of Nanning's most representative historical and cultural landmarks.

◈ Millennia-old Cycads Blossom Anew

Plant Kingdom

Qingxiu Mountain features several specialized plant gardens, among which the Cycad Garden and Palm Garden are the most renowned. The Cycad Garden houses over a hundred species of cycads from around the world, making it one of the largest cycad-specialized gardens in China. These ancient plants, known as "living fossils," thrive here.

The Palm Garden brings together various palm species, with towering palm trees complementing the tropical ambiance. Other attractions include the Heavenly Lake and Guanyin Buddhist Temple. The Heavenly Lake shimmers like a mirror, surrounded by lush greenery, while the Guanyin Temple is filled with the scent of incense and the serene sound of Buddhist chants.
