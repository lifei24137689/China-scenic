Millennia-Old Town · Dreamlike Home

Huangyao Ancient Town is located in Zhaoping County, Hezhou City, Guangxi. It originated during the Kaibao period of the Song Dynasty, with a history of nearly a thousand years. Nestled amidst karst peaks and featuring exquisite layout surrounded by mountains and rivers, it is renowned as a "Dreamlike Home." Blue stone paths, ancient bridges over flowing water, brick carvings and wood engravings — every detail showcases the unique charm of this ancient Lingnan town.

Over 300 well-preserved Lingnan-style residential buildings from the Ming and Qing dynasties, along with 8 crisscrossing blue stone streets, create a poetic picture of "small bridges over flowing streams." With its intact ancient architectural cluster and rich cultural atmosphere, Huangyao Ancient Town was rated as a National 5A Scenic Area in 2022.

Blue Stone Ancient Alley · Traces of Time

The blue stone streets are the soul of Huangyao Ancient Town. All eight main streets are paved with blue stone slabs. After centuries of wind and rain, the stone surfaces have been polished smooth as mirrors by countless footsteps. The Lingnan-style residences lining the streets are simple yet elegant, with upturned eaves, carved window lattices — every detail tells stories of bygone years.

The Guo Family Mansion is one of the most representative ancient buildings in Huangyao. It features a classic Lingnan courtyard layout with blue bricks, black tiles, and exquisite wood carvings. The courtyard's patios, corridors, and halls are meticulously arranged, showcasing the living scenes and cultural tastes of wealthy Lingnan families during the Ming and Qing dynasties.

By Dailong Bridge · Mountains and Water Reflect

Dailong Bridge is the iconic landmark of Huangyao Ancient Town. Built during the Ming Dynasty, it spans across the Yao River. This three-arch stone bridge features a simple yet elegant form, with vines winding around its body, creating a delightful contrast with the clear water below. The ancient banyan trees by the bridge, with their towering canopies, together with the old street and flowing water, form the most representative scene of Huangyao.

The Immortal Ancient Well is another wonder of Huangyao Ancient Town. Its water is clear, sweet, and flows year-round without cease. The well consists of five pools with different functions, used respectively for drinking, washing vegetables, laundry, and more, reflecting the wisdom of ancient water usage. The old trees and stone steps beside the well add a touch of antiquity and vitality to this millennia-old town.
