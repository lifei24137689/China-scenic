/// 01

Nature's Palette

Rainbow Danxia is located in Zhangye City, Gansu Province, and is one of China's most magnificent Danxia landforms. This colorful hilly terrain was formed during the Cretaceous period, 135 to 65 million years ago. After hundreds of millions of years of geological transformation, it has created the uniquely layered and brilliantly colored landscape seen today.

The core area of the scenic spot covers 4 km², with a total area of 50 km² and an average elevation of 1,820 meters. Red, yellow, orange, green, white, gray, and purple — seven colors intertwine like a giant natural oil painting, earning it the reputation of "God's overturned palette."

/// 02

An Epic of Color Forged Over Eons

Every color of Rainbow Danxia records Earth's history. Red represents iron-oxide-rich ancient climates, yellow symbolizes deposits from arid environments, and green hints at once-humid periods. These colorful strata read like an open history book, chronicling hundreds of millions of years of planetary transformation.

In 2020, Rainbow Danxia was officially designated a National 5A-level Tourist Attraction, and that same year it was listed as a UNESCO Global Geopark. This international recognition marks the geological and aesthetic value of Rainbow Danxia as world-class.

/// 03

A Photographer's Sanctuary of Light

Rainbow Danxia is a paradise for photography enthusiasts. Every morning and evening, when sunlight strikes the colorful hills at an angle, the Danxia colors transform through different layers of depth and intensity depending on the light's angle. At sunrise and sunset, golden rays spill over the red-and-orange interlocking ridges — an unforgettable spectacle.

In 2024, Rainbow Danxia received 3.4864 million visitors, setting a new record. The scenic area continues to improve its touring facilities and viewing platforms, ensuring every visitor can admire the most beautiful side of Danxia from the best vantage points. Whether professional photographers or casual tourists, everyone can find their own color story here.
