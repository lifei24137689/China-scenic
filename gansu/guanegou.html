/// 01

A Landscape Scroll of Longshang

Located in Longnan City, Gansu Province, Guanegou Scenic Area consists of three major sections: Guanzhu Valley, E'man Valley, and Leigu Mountain, covering a core area of 66 km². With dense forests, mirror-like lakes, and cascading waterfalls, it is renowned as the "Jiangnan of Longshang".

The average elevation of the scenic area exceeds 2,400 meters, with the highest peak, Leigu Mountain, reaching 4,154 meters and the lowest point at 1,748 meters. This vast vertical drop creates a rich diversity of natural landscape zones.

/// 02

Guanzhu, E'man & Leigu Peak

Guanzhu Valley is famous for its waterfalls — over a dozen of various sizes and forms. E'man Valley boasts stunning lakes and mountain scenery, with a heavenly lake as clear as a mirror reflecting the snowy peaks. Leigu Mountain, at 4,154 meters, is the highest peak in Longnan and is covered in snow year-round.

These three scenic areas together encompass diverse landforms including mountains, forests, lakes, waterfalls, and snow-capped peaks. In July 2022, Guanegou was officially designated as a 5A National Tourist Attraction, and by 2025, it received over 1 million visitors.

An Ecological Wonderland of Natural Oxygen

With a forest coverage rate of 75.1% and an extremely high concentration of negative oxygen ions, Guanegou is a true natural oxygen bar. The scenic area preserves a well-maintained primeval forest ecosystem, rich in diverse flora and fauna.

In 2025, the scenic area welcomed over 1 million visitors, making it one of the most popular tourist destinations in southern Gansu — a model of harmony between humans and nature.
