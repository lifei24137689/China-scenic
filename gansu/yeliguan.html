Red Cliff Valley · Danxia Wonder

Red Cliff Valley is one of the core attractions of Yeliguan Scenic Area, renowned for its magnificent Danxia landform. Over millions of years, wind and rain have sculpted the red sandstone into steep crimson cliffs, unique pinnacles, and deep gorges, like a red scar torn open on the earth.

Dense vegetation and babbling brooks fill the valley, where red cliffs and lush forests create a stunning contrast. Strolling through, you can feel the wonders of nature while experiencing the unique charm of the highland Danxia landscape.

Heavenly Lake · Sacred Lake Charm

Heavenly Lake Ye is nestled atop a mountain at 2,610 meters above sea level, the most famous alpine lake of Yeliguan. Its crystal-clear blue waters are surrounded by rolling mountains, carpet-like meadows dotted with yaks and sheep—a true "Jewel of Gannan."

For the Tibetan people, Heavenly Lake Ye holds sacred significance. Prayer flags flutter along its shores, and mani stone piles are scattered everywhere. The clear lake mirrors the blue sky, white clouds, and distant snow-capped mountains, creating a breathtaking highland tableau.

Highland Meadow · Eco Paradise

The Yeliguan Scenic Area integrates alpine lakes, Danxia landforms, and subalpine meadows, making it one of the most biodiverse regions in Gannan. The vast subalpine meadows change colors with the seasons—lush green in spring and summer, golden yellow in autumn.

This is also a paradise for wildlife. Under the blue sky and white clouds, yaks and sheep leisurely graze across the meadows while eagles soar overhead. The fresh air, magnificent scenery, and simple local customs make every visitor feel nature's generous gift.
