/// 01

Su Shi's Xuzhou West Lake

Yunlong Lake lies in the southern part of Xuzhou city, embraced by mountains on three sides and facing the city on the fourth. The lake surface stretches vast and misty, with hazy mountain hues. When Su Shi served as the prefect of Xuzhou, he managed flood control here and left behind the timeless verse: "Beneath Yunlong Mountain, I try on spring clothes; before the Crane Release Pavilion, I see off the setting sun."

Yunlong Lake and Hangzhou's West Lake are sister lakes, connected by the cultural legacy of Su Shi. With a surface area of 13 square kilometers, Yunlong Lake is slightly larger than West Lake. Its scenic beauty is no less impressive, earning it the praise: "Not West Lake, yet surpassing West Lake."

/// 02

Yunlong Mountain Views

Yunlong Mountain winds like a dragon along the eastern shore of Yunlong Lake, with nine connected peaks, hence its name "Nine-Segment Yunlong." The mountain is home to numerous historical sites including the Crane Release Pavilion, Xinghua Chan Temple, Dashi Cliff, and Yunlong Academy, making it a renowned cultural mountain of Xuzhou.

The Crane Release Pavilion was built by Hermit Zhang (Zhang Tianji). Su Shi often visited here to drink and compose poetry, writing the famous "Record of the Crane Release Pavilion." Ascending to the summit of Yunlong Mountain, you can overlook Yunlong Lake — a vast expanse of blue waves stretching as far as the eye can see, the most beautiful urban landscape in Xuzhou.

/// 03

Lakeside Stroll

Lakeside Park stretches along Yunlong Lake, shaded by lush green trees and adorned with blooming flowers. Su Gong Pagoda stands by the lake, built to commemorate Su Shi's flood control achievements. The Aqua World is a paradise for children, while the concert hall hosts Xuzhou's cultural and artistic events.

In spring, the apricot blossoms along Yunlong Lake burst into dazzling bloom; in summer, lotus flowers compete to open across the lake; in autumn, the mountain slopes are dyed in layers of color; in winter, the snow-covered scenery is serene and elegant. Through the changing seasons, Yunlong Lake remains the most beloved recreational haven for the people of Xuzhou.
