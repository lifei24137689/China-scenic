01 / Landscape Garden

Tianmu Lake Landscape Garden — The Gentle Beauty of Jiangnan's Pearl

Named after the Tianmu Mountain range, Tianmu Lake is surrounded by mountains with clear, sweet waters and winding shores. The Filial Piety Culture Garden, Scholar Culture Area, and Strange Stone Pavilion are scattered among the green hills. Boating on the lake, you can gaze at the misty mountains in the distance and admire the vast emerald waves up close — like a Jiangnan ink painting.

02 / Bamboo Sea

Nanshan Bamboo Sea — A Green Ocean of 35,000 Mu of Bamboo

Nanshan Bamboo Sea covers 35,000 mu of verdant bamboo, an endless green ocean of waves swaying with the wind — a spectacular "Bamboo Sea" landscape. Stroll along the bamboo forest boardwalk, breathe the fresh air rich in negative oxygen ions, and listen to the rustling of bamboo leaves in the wind. It is the perfect place to return to nature and cleanse your soul.

03 / Hot Spring & Cuisine

Yushui Hot Spring — East China's Premier Hot Spring Resort

Yushui Hot Spring is one of the best hot springs in East China, with mineral-rich waters. Relax in open-air pools surrounded by bamboo groves, enjoying the warmth of the spring and the tranquility of the bamboo sea. After soaking, savor Tianmu Lake's fish head clay pot — slow-cooked with natural Tianmu Lake bighead carp, featuring a milky-white broth with tender, flavorful meat. It is a nationally renowned delicacy.
