◈ Satellite Briefing Global Dinosaur City, located in Xinbei District, Changzhou, is a comprehensive tourist resort themed around dinosaurs, integrating science education, amusement, performances, accommodation, and shopping. It is known as the "Jurassic of the East".

/// 01

Jurassic World

The core attraction of Global Dinosaur City, China Dinosaur Park, opened in September 2000. It is a comprehensive theme park that perfectly combines museums, high-tech audio-visual effects, film special effects, and multimedia networks. The park revolves around the dinosaur theme, creating a rich Jurassic atmosphere.

The park features seven themed zones: China Dinosaur Museum, Lubra, Cooksuk Canyon, Magic Rainforest, Dream Manor, Adventure Port, and Hip-Hop Dinosaur City, with over 50 extreme amusement rides, allowing visitors to experience the charm of dinosaur culture through thrilling excitement.

Dinosaur Museum

China Dinosaur Museum is the landmark building of Dinosaur Park and one of the most concentrated museums for collecting and displaying dinosaur fossils in China. The museum exhibits over 50 dinosaur fossil skeletons from various geological eras, including Yongchuanosaurus, Mamenchisaurus, and Shandongosaurus.

Taking the origin, evolution, and extinction of paleontology as its narrative thread, the museum vividly recreates the Earth's environment during the age of dinosaurs through high-tech means and audio-visual technology. It also features a 4D motion theater, immersing visitors in the awe-inspiring dinosaur world.

Extreme Rides

Global Dinosaur City features numerous world-class amusement rides. The Crazy Fire Dragon Drill is the tallest swinging ride in Asia, the Babel Tower is the tallest drop tower in Asia, the Jurassic Adventure is a large indoor roller coaster, and the Hydro-Fire Power is the first dual-experience ride of its kind in China.

Beyond the thrilling rides, Dinosaur City also offers supporting facilities such as Dinosaur Valley Hot Spring, Dino Water Town Commercial Street, and the Dinosaur City Grand Theater. During summer and holidays, the park hosts large-scale themed performances and float parades, creating a spectacular spectacle.
