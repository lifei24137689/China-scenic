Premier Waterfall of China

Huangguoshu Waterfall is located in Zhenning Buyi and Miao Autonomous County, Anshun City, Guizhou Province. At 77.8 meters high and 101 meters wide, it is the largest waterfall in China and one of the world's famous waterfalls. The surging Baishui River plunges from a 70-meter cliff into Rhinoceros Pool, creating a thunderous roar audible for miles.

Over 300 years ago, geographer Xu Xiake marveled: "Water cascades over the rocks like mist rising to the sky, a sight of unparalleled grandeur." The waterfall transforms with the seasons — during the wet season it thunders like ten thousand horses galloping; in the dry season it hangs like a pearl curtain, elegant and ethereal.

Tianxing Bridge · Water Forest

Tianxing Bridge Scenic Area is located about 6 km downstream from the main waterfall, covering a planned area of 7 km². It integrates mountains, water, forests, and caves in one. With dense stone pillars and lush vegetation, it is hailed as "a ten-thousand-square-meter bonsai carved by wind and water."

Strolling along the three-kilometer stone path, you weave through stone walls, crevices, and grottoes. The Silver Chain Pool Waterfall cascades like thousands of silver chains into a deep pool. The Water Forest features water flowing among rocks, rocks standing in water, and trees growing atop rocks. The Tianxing Cave dazzles with stalactites in myriad forms.

Doupotang · Journey to the West

Doupotang Waterfall is located 1 km upstream from Huangguoshu Waterfall. With a crest width of 105 meters and a height of 21 meters, it is the widest waterfall crest in the Huangguoshu cluster. At its top lies a giant dissolution pool covering about 15,000 m², where water spreads across a 100-meter-long calcified shoal, graceful as a bride's veil.

This is also the filming location for the ending theme of the 1986 TV series Journey to the West. Whenever the melody of "Dare to Ask Where the Road Leads" plays, the image of the Tang Monk and his disciples crossing the waterfall comes to mind — an eternal classic in the hearts of generations.
