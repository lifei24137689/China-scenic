Jade Pool Wonderland · Pearl of Tianshan

Heavenly Lake of Tianshan, known as the "Jade Pool" in ancient times, is located on the northern slope of Bogda Peak at an elevation of 1,910 meters. The lake has a crescent shape with an area of approximately 4.9 square kilometers. Its waters are crystal clear, surrounded by mountains with snow-capped peaks reflected on the surface, earning it the title "Pearl of Tianshan."

The scenic area covers 548 square kilometers, centered around Heavenly Lake, integrating forests, grasslands, snow mountains, and cultural landmarks. It is a national 5A-level tourist attraction. Here, one can experience both the grandeur of northern landscapes and the elegance of southern waterways, with stunning scenery that changes across all four seasons.

/// 02

Snow Peaks Reflected in the Jade Pool

Heavenly Lake sits at an elevation of 1,910 meters, with a maximum depth of about 105 meters. It is an alpine barrier lake formed by glacial debris flows blocking the river channel. The lake water is clear and blue, reflecting the snow-capped Bogda Peak, creating a breathtaking alpine lake landscape.

The lakeshore is surrounded by spruce trees, lush grass, and colorful wildflowers. To the east lies East Little Heavenly Lake, and to the west, West Little Heavenly Lake, like two pearls adorning the area. The summer climate is cool and pleasant, making it an ideal summer resort; in winter, the snow-covered peaks stand tall against the frozen lake surface, offering a different kind of majestic beauty.

Deep in Tianshan · Ecological Sanctuary

The Heavenly Lake scenic area features a complete vertical natural zone spectrum, from desert steppe at the foothills to Schrenk's spruce forests on the mid-mountain slopes, and then to alpine meadows and snow belts, nurturing rich biodiversity. The Schrenk's spruce is a coniferous species unique to Tianshan, with tall, straight trunks that remain evergreen all year round.

The area is home to various rare wildlife species, including red deer, snow leopards, Siberian ibex, and golden eagles. The excellent ecological environment keeps the air fresh with extremely high negative oxygen ion levels, making it a natural oxygen bar. Heavenly Lake of Tianshan is not only a tourist destination but also an important ecological reserve.
