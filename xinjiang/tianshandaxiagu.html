GEOLOGICAL SURVEY

Northern Tianshan · Primeval Forest Sea

Tianshan Grand Canyon is located 48 km south of Urumqi city, and is the most complete virgin snow-capped spruce forest area on the northern slope of the Tianshan Mountains. Covering an area of 1,038 km² with an elevation of around 2,000 m, it integrates snow mountains, glaciers, forests, grasslands, and lakes. It was rated a national 5A scenic spot in 2014.

It features the most complete vertical natural landscape belt on the northern slope of the Tianshan Mountains, from foothill grasslands to snow-capped spruce forests, then to alpine meadows and glacier snow zones, forming a magnificent three-dimensional ecological panorama. Zhaobi Mountain stands majestically like a natural screen, guarding this secret realm.

NATURAL LANDMARK

Zhaobi Mountain Screen · Swan Lake Glimmer

Zhaobi Mountain is the iconic landscape of Tianshan Grand Canyon. Its massive body stretches across the canyon entrance like a giant natural screen, towering and magnificent. The steep cliffs and rugged rocks contrast beautifully with the surrounding snowy spruce forests, creating a breathtaking landscape painting.

Swan Lake lies deep in the canyon, with crystal-clear blue rippling waters. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and reflected spruce trees, it resembles an emerald embedded in the Tianshan range. In summer, swans play on the lake; in winter, it freezes over like a mirror — each season has its own charm.

ALPINE MEADOW

Qiaoya Meadow · Spruce Wonderland

Qiaoya Meadow is an alpine meadow area within Tianshan Grand Canyon, with lush green grass and wildflowers everywhere. In summer, the meadow turns emerald green, dotted with grazing cattle and sheep, forming a stunning alpine pasture picture with distant snow mountains and spruce forests. It is a paradise for hikers and photographers.

The Tianshan Dam stretches across the canyon, creating a spectacular alpine lake landscape. Jas Daban is the main passage leading deep into the canyon, with ever-changing scenery along the way — each step reveals a different view. The virgin forest is filled with towering ancient trees and fresh air, with extremely high negative oxygen ion content, making it a true natural oxygen bar.
