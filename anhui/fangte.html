I · Theme Park Cluster

Four Parks Joy Matrix

Located in the Jiujiang District of Wuhu City, Anhui Province, the Wuhu Fantawild Tourist Resort covers a total area of approximately 5 square kilometers. It is the largest Fantawild theme park cluster in China and the only Fantawild park to be rated as a National 5A Tourist Attraction. Each of the four theme parks has its own distinctive features, forming a super cultural tourism matrix where technology and culture interweave.

Since the opening of the first park in 2008, Fantawild has adhered to the core philosophy of "Culture + Technology," deeply integrating traditional Chinese culture with modern high-tech to create immersive amusement experiences that meet international standards while retaining Eastern charm. Having received over 67.72 million visitors in total and attracting more than 5 million visitors annually, it stands as a benchmark in China's theme park industry.

II · Oriental Divine Painting

National Trend Tech Chinese Legend

Fantawild Oriental Heritage, grandly opened in 2015, is the first chapter of Fantawild's "Beautiful China" trilogy. Centered on China's 5,000-year history and culture, the park utilizes laser multimedia, 3D effects, miniature real scenes, live shows and other high-tech methods to transform classic myths and legends such as Nüwa Mends the Sky, The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl, and Lady Meng Jiang into awe-inspiring immersive experiences.

The park features world-class rides like the Jungle Flying Dragon (wooden roller coaster), Leifeng Pagoda (indoor drop tower), and Blazing Winds & Clouds, perfectly combining traditional cultural IP with modern technology. Oriental Heritage is not just an amusement park, but a "living" museum of Chinese culture.

III · Joy World

Classic Rides Ultimate Experience

Fantawild Wonderland is the first park in Wuhu Fantawild, opened in 2008, with science fiction and interactive experiences as its greatest features. The park includes over a dozen large themed areas such as Sunshine Plaza, Fisherman's Wharf, Elf Valley, Mysterious River Valley, Vesuvius Volcano, and Dinosaur Crisis, covering themes of modern technology, scientific fantasy, myths and legends, history and culture, and children's amusement.

Fantawild Dream Kingdom (2010) incorporates trendy entertainment elements such as anime, cartoons, and film special effects. Classic projects like Water Overflows Golden Mountain, The Monkey King, and Adventure in Qin Mausoleum are deeply loved by family tourists. Fantawild Water Park (2014) is a premier water entertainment destination in East China, with an average daily reception of over 20,000 visitors in summer.
