Guanzhai Mountain Majestic & Precipitous

Covering 12.7 km², Guanzhai Mountain features early mature monoclinal Danxia landform. Its peak resembles the Xiezhi crown of ancient imperial censors, symbolizing uprightness and integrity. The area integrates mountains, waters, cliffs, springs, and temples, presenting a panorama of majesty, wonder, serenity, elegance, and sublimity.

It preserves over 40 ancient academies and cliff inscriptions from the Song and Yuan dynasties, and more than 100 unique landscapes including the Root of Life and the Gate of Life. Together with Wuyi Mountain, it is hailed as "Southern Zhai and Northern Yi — Twin Wonders of Danxia."

Shimen Lake Azure Water & Danxia

Shimen Lake is like an emerald set amidst the lofty peaks and wondrous valleys of Guanzhai Mountain. Water winds through the mountains, and peaks reflect in the water — its gentle grace contrasts beautifully with Guanzhai's rugged grandeur. Scattered along the shores are sights like Lotus Peak, Saddle Stockade, and Elephant Playing in Water.

Boating on the emerald waves feels like wandering through a painting — an ethereal experience. The "Gate of Life" at Shimen Lake echoes the "Root of Life" at Guanzhai Mountain, earning the praise: "Masculine majesty unmatched, feminine grace unrivaled under heaven."

Zhu'an Stockade Natural Moat Wonder

Zhu'an Stockade was a historically important military fortress, located 3 km north of Guanzhai Mountain. It features the "Heavenly Wall" rising a thousand fathoms, "Sky-Piercing Ridge" cliffs piercing the clouds, "Echo Steps" resonating with clarity, "Stone Moat Gate" impassable by thousands, "Twin Sword Peaks" stabbing the sky, and the lifelike "Old Immortal of the South."

The "Heavenly Lake" atop the mountain never overflows nor dries up year-round — a true wonder. Zhu'an Stockade connects to Nine-Dragon Lake, where mountains embrace waters, islands dot the surface, waterways weave through, and peaks tower above — an ideal retreat for leisure and vacation.
